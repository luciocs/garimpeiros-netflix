Discover the latest updates and news about Garimpeiros Netflix

"Garimpeiros da Netflix" is a group that was created with the purpose of digging through Netflix and recommending hidden gems - films or series that you might not have heard of or didn't think to watch. The group was originally formed on Facebook, and their recommendations are now available on Google Assistant as well. The aim is to save you from spending more time searching for something to watch than actually watching. The group consists of the best 'garimpeiros' (prospectors) who find the gold in Netflix's content and recommend the best films and series available on the streaming service.
